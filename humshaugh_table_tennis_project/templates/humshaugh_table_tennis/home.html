Welcome

Humshaugh Table Tennis Club (HTTC) has been formed to encourage and promote this healthy sport at all levels of ages (Over 10 years old) and abilities.

Our club meetings are held on most (but not all) Thursdays in Humshaugh Village Hall which is spacious and well lighted making it very suitable for this sport.

We are currently seeking new members from Humshaugh and the surrounding Tynedale Towns and Villages.

Please come and visit us to see the facilities and decide whether you are interested in joining the club.
